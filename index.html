Omlet

  • Time:until fully cooked
  • Serves: 1 per omlet
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2
Grated cheese desired amount
Butter 1 tsp
  • side note... you can purchse needed ingredients at Aldi's

Step 1:Beat two large eggs (or more) in a bowl, mug or jug

Step 2: Stir in plenty of your favourite grated cheese

Step 3:Add a knob of butter to a frying pan and heat on a high heat.

Step 4:Once butter is bubbling pour in the egg mix and allow to set for around 30-45 seconds.

Step 5:With a spatula, go around the omelette and lift up the edges off the bottom of the pan, allowing uncooked egg to run underneath.

Step 6:Once the eggs begin to firm up, fold the whole thing in half with the spatula. Alternatively you could fold both sides into the middle.

Step 7:Continue frying until golden.

Source: cheesy omlet recipe
